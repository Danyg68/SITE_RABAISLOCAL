‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Do I need to create a different account for each type of prospect?

A: No! You use the SAME referral number for all 3 pages. The system automatically knows what type of registration it is (merchant, consumer, or affiliate) thanks to hidden fields in the form.

Q: What happens after the prospect enters their email?

A: RabaisLocal automatically sends an email to the prospect with:

  • The personalized registration link (with your referral number)
  • Your contact details (name, phone, email) so they can contact you
  • Instructions to complete their registration

Q: How long is the registration link valid?

A: The link never expires. The prospect can sign up whenever they want, and the registration will still be credited to your account.

Q: Can I customize the pages?

A: No, the pages are standardized to ensure a consistent message. However, you can customize your introductory message when you send the link.

Q: Should I follow up with the prospect after they receive the email?

A: Yes, it is recommended! Send a message 24-48h later: "Did you have time to sign up? Do you have any questions?" This significantly increases the conversion rate.
