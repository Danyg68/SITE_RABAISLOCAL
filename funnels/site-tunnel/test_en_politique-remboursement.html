1) Policy Purpose

This policy defines the conditions for refund, cancellation, and renewal for all products and services offered by RabaisLocal and RabaisLocal Marketing, including consumer subscriptions, merchant plans, and affiliate kits.

2) Products and Services Covered

  • Affiliate Kit ($47 in pre-launch, $77 regular)

3) Refund Period

A member may request a refund of their purchase within 7 days following the payment date, provided that the service or content has not already been activated, delivered, or downloaded.

4) Non-Refundable Products and Content

The following digital products and instant services are non-refundable as soon as they are accessible:

  • The RabaisLocal Affiliate Kit (immediate access to tools, training, and benefits).
  • Ebooks, training, guides, or digital content delivered after purchase.
  • Subscriptions or plans already activated on the platform.

5) Renewal and Automatic Fees

RabaisLocal affiliates benefit from a unique model without debiting a bank card:

  • Monthly fees ($27/month) and annual renewal ($47) are automatically deducted from commissions generated.
  • No amount is debited from the member's credit card or personal account.
  • If the commission balance is insufficient, the account is simply suspended until regularization.

6) Refund Request Procedure

Any request must be sent by email to rabaislocal@rabaislocal.com specifying: your full name, registration email, the product concerned, and the purchase date. Valid requests are processed within 5 to 10 business days.
